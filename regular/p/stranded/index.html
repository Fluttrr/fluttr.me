stranded

stranded by the sea, eternally
sunsets slowly setting over me
day by day, tides wash in and out
left with nothing but a heart of doubt

tell me where the waves go
in the end, what does my heart know?
will my thoughts be heard?
am i but a lonesome lovebird?

words unspoken, forever lost to sea
will anybody ever look back at me?
my only hope, left by faint memories
spoiled by long-lost opportunities

winds roar over the shore, trying to hold on
yet my heart is weak, wishing i were home
making me ask why, oh why?
am i stranded by the sea, eternally
